Hudson Baby Unisex Muslin Cotton Sleep Sack

Crafted for comfort and safety, this sleep sack is designed to keep your little one cozy and secure. Made from 100% soft muslin cotton, it's gentle on delicate skin and provides a breathable, lightweight option for all-day use.
